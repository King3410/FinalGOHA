Company History

Founded in 2011, our school began with a vision to provide quality education for Kindergarten to Grade 6 students. Over the years, we expanded our facilities, integrated modern technology, and introduced extracurricular programs to support holistic growth. Today, we proudly serve as a cornerstone of our community, fostering young minds with excellence and care.

School Philosophy
Gate of Heaven Academy School of Caloocan is dedicated to providing a holistic education rooted in excellence, faith, and compassion. We strive to nurture young minds, instill moral values, and empower students to become responsible and service-oriented members of society.
To be a leading institution that shapes future leaders with strong moral values, academic excellence, and a commitment to faith and service, creating a community of lifelong learners dedicated to making a positive impact on society and the world.
• Integrity
• Compassion

• Service to Others
We believe that every child is a unique individual with God-given talents. Our philosophy centers on creating a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to discover their potential through holistic and meaningful learning experiences.
